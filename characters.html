Meet the Characters

The Chosen One

Ethan Blaze

"Mind of a genius, heart of a machine—power meets precision."

Ethan is extremely smart, with an IQ of 285—the highest in the world. He works as a software company manager and loves technology more than anything. As the story goes on, he starts turning into a robot, gaining powerful tech-based abilities.

Genius IQ Tech Transformation Strategic Mind Leadership
The Mystic

Tara Frost

"Where magic meets mystery, power finds its purpose."

Tara is a mysterious girl with a deep connection to ancient magic. She has the ability to see glimpses of the future and communicate with spirits. Her powers grow stronger as she learns more about her family's connection to the Tree of Life.

Future Sight Spirit Communication Ancient Magic Empathy
The Warrior

Jax Storm

"Strength forged in battle, heart tested by time."

Jax is a skilled warrior with a mysterious past. He has enhanced physical abilities and can manipulate energy. His loyalty to his friends and his determination to protect them make him a formidable ally.

Enhanced Strength Energy Manipulation Combat Mastery Loyalty
The Shadow

Nina Shade

"In darkness, she finds her light; in shadows, her true power."

Nina has the ability to manipulate shadows and darkness. She can become invisible and move through shadows. Her mysterious nature and complex past make her both an ally and an enigma.

Shadow Manipulation Invisibility Stealth Agility
